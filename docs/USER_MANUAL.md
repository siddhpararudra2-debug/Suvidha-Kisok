## Login Options

### Option 1: Aadhaar Login (Recommended)
1. Tap **"Aadhaar OTP"** tab
2. Enter your 12-digit Aadhaar number
3. Tap **"Send OTP"**
4. Enter the 6-digit OTP received on your mobile
5. Tap **"Verify & Login"**

### Option 2: Consumer ID Login
1. Tap **"Consumer ID"** tab
2. Select service type (Electricity/Gas/Water)
3. Enter your Consumer Number (found on your bill)
4. Tap **"Login"**

### Option 3: Guest Mode
1. Tap **"Continue as Guest"**
2. View bills and maps without login
3. Note: You cannot pay bills or register complaints as a guest

### Option 4: DigiLocker
1. Tap **"DigiLocker"** tab
2. You'll be redirected to DigiLocker
3. Login with your DigiLocker credentials
4. Allow SUVIDHA access

## Troubleshooting

### "OTP Not Received"
- Check your mobile network
- Ensure Aadhaar is linked to your mobile
- Wait 60 seconds and tap **"Resend OTP"**

### "Payment Failed"
- Check your internet connection
- Verify UPI ID or card details
- Try a different payment method
- Contact your bank if amount was debited

### "Location Not Found"
- Allow location permission in browser
- Move away from walls/obstructions
- Enter address manually instead

### "Session Timed Out"
- For security, sessions expire after 3 minutes of inactivity
- Simply log in again
